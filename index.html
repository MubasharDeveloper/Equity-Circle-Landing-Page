Obsess over feeling your best

Every day is different with Equity Circle because your body is different every day. Because Equity Circle is calibrated specifically to you, it knows exactly how to coach you towards the best sleeps, workouts, recoveries, and endeavors you’ve ever pursued. And we obsess over building the world’s best wearable so that you can obsess over what matters most: you.

Explore The Data

Get Better Sleep

Quality sleep doesn’t just mean more of it. Equity Circle tracks all four phases of your sleep, calculates your sleep efficiency, and even recommends bed and wake times to help you strategize your best night’s sleep, every night.

Strain Smarter

Whether you’re on a hike or nervous before a flight, everything you do in a day contributes to stress on your body. Your Equity Circle Strain Score measures that load, and helps you avoid the possibility of overtraining, or encourages you to up your game and push harder the next day.

Recover Faster

Proper rest, hydration, and other healing habits contribute to your daily recovery. Your Equity Circle Recovery Score calculates how efficiently your body is balancing itself out after stress, so you know when to take on more, and when to dial it back.

Manage Your Stress

Equity Circle measures your stress in real-time, and offers breathwork protocols designed to help you regulate. With Stress Monitor, you’ll discover your personal triggers, see patterns, and begin to feel more control over your stress levels.

Customize What You Track

It’s not just hard workouts and fatigue that influence how your body performs. Equity Circle is the only wearable with a behavior journal that allows you to choose over 140 daily habits to track, and calculates exactly how they help or hurt you.
